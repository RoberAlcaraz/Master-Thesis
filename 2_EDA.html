Description and visualization of the variables

Our variables are:

  • Indicative variables:

    • ID
    • key

  • Categorical variables (10 + 1):

    • mortality (target)
    • period
    • cover
    • sex
    • smoker
    • good_health
    • capital_factor_1
    • capital_factor_2
    • capital_factor_3
    • IMC_factor_1
    • IMC_factor_2

  • Numerical variables (7):

    • actuarial_age
    • sus_age
    • exp
    • duration
    • duration_cat
    • capital
    • IMC
